## Hands-On: Leonardo AI
Leonardo AI is the platform I go to when I’m not exactly sure what I want and I need to explore a "vibe." It’s a powerhouse for artistic experimentation. Features like the Motion toggle (for turning static images into clips) or the ability to train your own LoRAs make it a compelling choice for concept artists and game designers.

However, in my daily workflow, I found myself getting frustrated by the **Ideogram vs Leonardo AI** gap in text rendering. Leonardo often defaults to "aesthetic blur" when text is involved, which requires me to pull the image into Photoshop to manually correct the characters. If you are doing architectural visualization or abstract character design, Leonardo is fantastic. But if you are doing corporate design, the "Leonardo look" can sometimes feel a bit too artificial or cluttered compared to the clean, crisp output of Ideogram.
