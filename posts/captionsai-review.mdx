## What is Captions.ai?
In the crowded space of **AI video editing**, Captions.ai stands out by focusing on a singular mission: making short-form video creation accessible and fast. Unlike traditional NLEs (Non-Linear Editors) that require a deep understanding of timelines and keyframes, Captions.ai uses machine learning to handle the heavy lifting.

As a developer, I spend most of my day working within the clean, logic-driven environment of Claude. When I switch to video editing, I don’t want to learn complex software; I want something that respects my time. Captions.ai effectively automates the grunt work of generating captions, trimming dead air, and even correcting eye movement, allowing creators to focus on the message rather than the pixels.

## FAQ

**1. Is Captions.ai better than using Claude to write scripts?**
They serve different purposes. Claude is for the logic and structure of your script, while Captions.ai is for the post-production visuals. Using both is the winning strategy for **video content production**.

**2. Can I use my own voice for AI dubbing?**
Yes. Captions.ai offers voice cloning, which allows you to record a short sample and then apply your synthesized voice to translations, ensuring your brand identity remains consistent across languages.

**3. Does Captions.ai support multi-track editing?**
It supports basic layering, but it isn't an NLE. You aren't going to be doing complex multi-cam edits here. Keep it to one or two layers for the best experience.

**4. Is there a free version available?**
There is a limited free trial, but for any serious **creator workflow**, you will need to commit to one of the paid tiers to remove watermarks and access the high-quality export features.
