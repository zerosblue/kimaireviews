## What is ElevenLabs?
ElevenLabs is a research-first AI audio platform specializing in text-to-speech (TTS), voice cloning, and AI dubbing. Since its inception, it has moved from being a simple "read-my-text" tool to a comprehensive suite for content creators, game developers, and enterprise audio production.

As a developer in Seoul, I’ve seen my share of "AI-in-a-box" tools. Most fail the moment the script requires a pause, a whisper, or a specific dialect. ElevenLabs approaches audio differently; they focus on the "prosody"—the rhythm, stress, and intonation of speech—rather than just stacking phonemes together. It is a powerful engine for anyone who needs to bridge the gap between written content and human-sounding audio.

## Hands-On Experience
In my 18 years in CRM and Sales Ops, I’ve used a variety of automated communication tools. Usually, these sound robotic, which kills engagement. When I started testing ElevenLabs for some internal training videos and localized content, the difference was immediate.

My standard workflow involves using Claude to draft scripts. I’ll prompt Claude: *"Write a 60-second instructional script about our new CRM workflow, keeping the tone professional yet conversational."* Once I have the text, I paste it into ElevenLabs.

The **Speech-to-Speech** feature is where I’ve found the most value. Sometimes, the text-to-speech engine struggles with the specific technical jargon of our sales software. Instead of fighting the settings, I record myself saying the lines with the correct emphasis and cadence. I then upload that file into ElevenLabs and tell it to output using a high-stability, professional voice profile. The result is indistinguishable from a human recording.

However, it isn’t perfect. I’ve found that the "Professional Voice Cloning" process is time-consuming. You need clean audio, and even then, if you aren't careful with your source input, you can get weird artifacts. I also find the moderation layer to be a bit aggressive—I’ve had legitimate internal documents flagged because they contained sensitive-sounding financial data, which required manual review to clear. If you are using this for production pipelines, factor in a bit of "buffer time" for those random moderation hitches.

Compared to other tools, ElevenLabs feels like a "pro" tool. Where other AI voice generators feel like toys, this feels like an integrated piece of a production stack.
