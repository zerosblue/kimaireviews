## What is Reclaim AI?

Rather than you manually dragging blocks of "Focus Time," Reclaim AI syncs with your tasks, project management tools like Jira, and your habits to find optimal slots for you. As someone who has spent 18+ years in Sales Ops, I’ve seen many tools promise "automated scheduling," but Reclaim AI is different because it understands **dynamic priority**. If a high-priority meeting gets dropped into your calendar, Reclaim AI automatically identifies which of your scheduled "Deep Work" sessions to reschedule and moves them to the next best availability.

## FAQ

**1. Does Reclaim AI work with Outlook calendars?**
As of 2026, Reclaim AI is primarily built for Google Calendar users. If your work relies exclusively on the Microsoft ecosystem, you will not be able to use the core automation features.

**2. Will it delete my existing meetings?**
No. Reclaim AI only modifies the blocks it creates (Tasks, Habits, and Focus Time). It will never delete or move an event that you have manually created or accepted from an external invite unless you have explicitly configured it to manage your "non-meeting" time.

**3. Is it too aggressive with scheduling?**
It can be, especially at the start. I recommend setting your "Working Hours" strictly and using the "Buffer" settings to ensure it doesn't pack your day too tightly. You are always in control of the configuration.

**4. How does it handle my private tasks?**
Reclaim AI respects your privacy. You can label tasks as "Private," and they will appear as simple "Busy" blocks on your calendar to colleagues, hiding the actual nature of the work being done.
