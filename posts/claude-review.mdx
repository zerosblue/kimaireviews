## Quick Verdict

Claude is my daily driver — and has been for 18 months. For developers who write code, review PRs, and work with large documents, it's the most capable AI assistant available in 2026. The 200K context window and reasoning quality are genuinely in a class of their own.

## What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic, a safety-focused AI company founded by former OpenAI researchers. The flagship model, Claude 3.7 Sonnet, excels at complex reasoning, code generation, and working with large amounts of text — all in a single conversation.

## Key Features

- **200K context window**: Paste an entire codebase, legal document, or research paper — Claude handles it
- **Projects**: Persistent workspaces where Claude remembers your files and instructions
- **Artifacts**: Claude generates code, documents, and diagrams as live, interactive outputs
- **Claude.ai desktop app**: Works natively on Mac/Windows with screen context
- **Honest uncertainty**: Claude says "I'm not sure" instead of confidently making things up

## Hands-On Experience

I use Claude for everything: debugging production code, writing this blog, drafting client proposals, and reviewing SQL migrations. Here's what 18 months of daily use has taught me:

**Code review**: This is where Claude beats every competitor. I can paste 500 lines of TypeScript and ask "what could go wrong in production?" — and get a structured analysis that catches edge cases I missed. It thinks like a senior developer, not a documentation chatbot.

**Large document analysis**: I fed Claude an 80-page vendor contract and asked it to flag clauses that create business risk. It identified 12 issues, ranked by severity. This would have taken a lawyer 4+ hours.

**Writing**: Clear, direct, and adapts to your voice. I give it a rough outline and it fills in with real substance — no filler, no excessive hedging.

**Where it falls short**: No image generation. If you need DALL-E-style output, you'll need a separate tool. The plugin/integration ecosystem is also smaller than OpenAI's.

## FAQ

**Is Claude free to use?**
Yes — Claude.ai has a free tier with access to Claude 3.5 Haiku and limited Claude 3.7 Sonnet usage.

**Claude vs ChatGPT: which is better for coding?**
Claude wins on complex code review, debugging, and architecture. ChatGPT has a larger plugin ecosystem and image generation.

**What is the context window?**
Claude Pro offers a 200K token context window — roughly 150,000 words. You can paste entire codebases or lengthy documents in one conversation.

**Can I use Claude for my app?**
Yes — Anthropic offers an API. See the Claude API documentation at anthropic.com/api.
