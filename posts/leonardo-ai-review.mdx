## FAQ

### 1. Is Leonardo AI better than Midjourney for professionals?
For developers and designers who need a structured workspace and reproducible results, yes. Midjourney is excellent for art, but Leonardo AI provides better control features (like Image Guidance) and a web-based dashboard that fits into a professional workflow.

### 2. Can I use Leonardo AI for commercial projects?
Yes. Depending on your subscription plan, you own the commercial rights to the images you generate. Always check their latest Terms of Service, but as of this **Leonardo AI review**, they are very developer-friendly regarding intellectual property.

### 3. Does Leonardo AI support text generation within images?
It is getting much better at rendering text, but it’s still not a substitute for a dedicated graphic design tool like Figma or Photoshop. For simple UI labels and button text, it works well, but complex typography usually requires post-processing.

### 4. How does the credit system work?
You get a fixed amount of tokens every month based on your subscription. Different tasks—like high-res upscaling, using custom LoRAs, or standard generations—consume different amounts of credits. If you run out, you have to wait for the reset or purchase a booster pack.
