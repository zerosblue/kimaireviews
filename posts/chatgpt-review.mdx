## Quick Verdict

ChatGPT remains the most recognized AI assistant in 2026, but it's no longer the obvious choice for developers. GPT-4o is impressive, yet Claude 3.7 now outperforms it on complex reasoning tasks. For most everyday writing and coding tasks, ChatGPT is a safe, reliable pick — just don't expect it to be the best at everything.

## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is OpenAI's flagship AI assistant, powered by the GPT-4o model. It handles text, images, audio, and code within a single conversation. Since its launch in late 2022, it has become the most-used AI chatbot on the planet with over 180 million weekly active users.

## Key Features

- **GPT-4o**: OpenAI's latest multimodal model — fast, capable, and free (with limits)
- **Custom GPTs**: Build your own AI assistants tailored to specific tasks
- **Memory**: Remembers your preferences across sessions
- **Code Interpreter**: Run Python code, analyze files, create charts
- **Plugins & API**: Extensive third-party integration ecosystem

## Hands-On Experience

I've been using ChatGPT alongside Claude for the past 18 months. Here's what I found in practice:

**Where it shines**: Brainstorming, drafting email copy, explaining concepts at different skill levels. The Custom GPTs feature is genuinely useful — I built one for writing CRM playbooks that saves me about 2 hours per week.

**Where it struggles**: Deep code review and debugging. On several occasions I asked ChatGPT to explain complex SQL logic and it gave plausible-sounding but incorrect answers. Claude handles this significantly better in my experience.

**The UI**: Clean and fast. The new sidebar for organizing chats is a welcome improvement. The mobile app is one of the best among AI assistants.

## Pricing Breakdown

For solo developers, the free tier handles occasional use well. The Plus plan is worth it if you're using it for client work.

## Who Should Use ChatGPT?

- Non-technical users who need a gentle AI onboarding experience
- Marketers and content teams who need image generation + writing in one tool
- Developers who want the largest plugin/integration ecosystem
- Teams already on the OpenAI API stack

## Alternatives to Consider

- **Claude (Anthropic)**: Better reasoning, longer context, stronger coding — my personal primary tool
- **Gemini Advanced**: Better Google Workspace integration
- **Cursor / GitHub Copilot**: Dedicated coding environments

## Final Rating: 4.2 / 5

ChatGPT is excellent — it just has more competition now. The ecosystem and brand recognition are still unmatched, but for raw capability, Claude 3.7 edges it out on technical tasks.

## FAQ

**Is ChatGPT free?**
Yes — GPT-4o is free with daily usage limits. No credit card required.

**ChatGPT vs Claude: which is better for coding?**
In my testing, Claude handles complex debugging and code reasoning better. ChatGPT is more consistent for quick scripts and boilerplate.

**Does ChatGPT remember previous conversations?**
Yes — with the Memory feature enabled (Plus plan), it remembers details you share across sessions.

**Is ChatGPT safe for business use?**
OpenAI offers business plans with data privacy controls. Free and Plus plans use your data to train models by default — you can opt out in settings.
